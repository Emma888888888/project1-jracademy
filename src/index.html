About Us

FutureAus Asset Management is an integrated financial platform. It provides clients with a wide range of investment opportunities in real estate, private equity, liquid and fixed asset markets as well as fund management with one stop tailored services to High-Net-Worth Individuals and institution.

Our experienced team understands its client's investment objectives and is composed of experts who select investment assets that can achieve returns within a controlled risk. We also understand that it is not only markets that may change, but also clients risk objectives. As such, the team conducts periodic asset allocation reviews with its clients to set and assess progress on objectives while maintaining a balance between the client's preferred risk and return. It is a subsidiary of FutureAus Asset Management Group.

Our Vision

We aim to be the premier source of investment advice and asset management services for global-minded investors. We want to stand out from the crowd by committing ourselves to providing high-quality investment opportunities, advice, and tailor-made asset placement solutions, aligned with our core values and the aspirations of our clients.

Our Values

In a changing, globalised world, FutureAus Group seeks to promote international and intercultural exchange. We place high value in integrity, respect, with understanding and collaboration in all aspects of our business. We value people above all else. We devote ourselves to maintaining the highest standards of service, regulation and care for our clients, whilst also providing an inspiring and supportive workplace for our employees.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Social responsibility and sustainable growth are always at the core of FutureAus’ business values. We work closely with local governments to provide foreign direct investment and help local communities to grow and prosper. FutureAus is also committed to helping boost local economies and living standards by investing in healthcare, education and infrastructure which, in turn, will create a sustainable growth model that can support greater local employment and spending.
